Opening of a New Rezet Store in Sweden

Rezet Store er a leading Danish sneaker destination og har been in business since 2011, hvor we have shared our passion for sneakers med loyal customers og dedicated sneakerheads. Vores first physical store opened i 2011 i Rådhusstræde i inner Copenhagen – og i dag we have five stores in the største Danish cities. A combination af a sleek design concept, strong community in the sneaker milieu, a carefully curated selection of sneakers, og a big focus on the local communities, hvor our stores are located, have contributed to making Rezet Store the preferred choice når it comes til sneakers.

Store Universe

We are excited to announce the opening of our new Rezet Store in Sweden! Efter many years of success in Denmark, we are expanding our presence and bringing our unique selection of sneakers and streetwear to our Swedish customers. The new store is centrally located in the heart of Stockholm and spans over 500 square meters. Med our passion for fashion and customer-focused service, we have created a space that is not just a store, but also a gathering point for sneaker enthusiasts and fashionistas..
